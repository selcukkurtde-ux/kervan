questions, answered

before you hitch

Will the hitch fit my bike?

Yes. The coupling mounts on the rear axle, and quick-release plus 12 mm thru-axle adapters ship attached. E-bikes pull it happily — the K3 actually prefers one.

What happens if it rains?

The shell is sealed to IP54 with a gasketed lid. Your sleeping bags, books and projector stay dry; the aluminum just looks better wet.

Deposit and damage — how does it work?

We hold €150 and release it Monday after inspection. Normal wear is ours — scratches are part of an aluminum life. We only charge for lost or broken modules, at the prices on the build page.

Where do you deliver?

Inside Munich, delivery and collection are included. Within 25 km of the ring it's €9 each way. Friday delivery lands 08:00–10:00; Sunday pickup runs 18:00–21:00.

Can I keep it past Sunday?

Extend any build for €19 a day — tap the link in your confirmation email before Sunday noon and we'll move the pickup.
