the material · why metal

soft trips, hard shell

Fabric trailers sag, flap and soak. We build the way luggage used to be built — and rent it so you never have to store it.

5052 aluminum, hand-riveted

Marine-grade sheet, rolled and riveted in the workshop. It dents before it breaks, and every dent has a story we're happy to keep.

Sealed against Bavaria

IP54 shell, gasketed lid, drainage channels. Summer storm on the Isar? Your books arrive home drier than you do.

Serviced between every rental

Bearings checked, rails cleaned, rivets inspected, shell washed. Each trailer carries a stamped service tag — read it before you roll.
